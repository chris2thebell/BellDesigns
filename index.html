Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We provide drafting services including floor plans, electrical plans, and 3D rendering. (COMING SOON) We also offer contracting services and construction consulting as needed and requested.

How long does a project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Typical drafting timelines are 24-48 hours to completion after measurements are taken. (COMING SOON) Contractor work will depend on the size of the project and desired timelines.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes! For drafting only, we include a set number of revisions to ensure client satisfaction. (COMING SOON) For contracted work, change orders are permitted, but limited and may be at an additional cost.
